Google I/O 2026: Gemini 3.5 Flash Launches + Always-On AI Agent Spark + Video Model Omni

Google I/O 2026 kicked off on May 20 in Mountain View, California, centered on a systematic pivot to "Agentic AI." Key announcements include: Gemini 3.5 Flash is now the default model for Gemini and Search AI modes, outperforming the previous 3.1 Pro on coding and agent benchmarks at 4x speed and under half the cost.

Cursor Ships Strongest Coding Model Yet — Composer 2.5 Built on Kimi K2.5, Musk Endorses

AI code editor Cursor officially launched Composer 2.5 on May 18, its most powerful proprietary coding model to date. Built on Moonshot AI's open-source Kimi K.2.5 architecture with deep optimization, training data increased 25x over the previous generation, with 85% of compute budget allocated to reinforcement learning.

In official benchmarks, Composer 2.5 approaches Claude Opus 4.7 level performance on long-task stability and complex instruction following. Elon Musk confirmed on X that the model partially leveraged the Colossus 2 supercomputing cluster for training.

This marks the evolution of AI coding tools from "model consumers" to "model developers." Cursor's choice to deeply customize on a Chinese open-source model validates the commercial viability of open-source ecosystems.

Matrix Ultra Unveils Flagship Humanoid Robot MATRIX-3: Starting at 580K RMB, Targeting L2 by Year-End

Matrix Ultra debuted its new flagship humanoid robot MATRIX-3 at its 2026 Tech Day: 170cm tall, 65kg, 4-hour battery life, 20-minute fast charge, powered by Nvidia AGX, featuring a 27-DOF dexterous hand and 3D biomimetic fabric "humanoid body."

Pricing: standard edition from 580,000 RMB (~$80K), PRO edition at 680,000 RMB. CEO Zhang Haixing noted that joints are the most expensive component (a single ball screw costs more than a MacBook), but industry cost reduction is extremely fast — 30-40% annually. Citing smartphone and automotive history, he predicts significant price drops within 3-8 years.

Zhang proposed an L1-L3 intelligence classification: currently at L1.5, targeting L2 (partial autonomy) by end of 2026 and L3 (mostly autonomous) by 2028. He emphasized that "China is already far ahead in robot body and hardware manufacturing," though North America maintains roughly a 3-month lead in model capabilities.

580K RMB pricing makes MATRIX-3 one of the most price-competitive flagship humanoid robots on the market
"Consumer companion robots will be very affordable within 3 years" — if true, explosive consumer market growth
AI investment exceeds 60% of budget, "unlimited investment in AI" — software, not hardware, is the core differentiator

Spirit AI and Bosch China Sign Strategic Partnership for Large-Scale Industrial Deployment of Embodied AI

On May 6, 2026, embodied intelligence company Spirit AI and Bosch China officially signed a strategic partnership agreement in Munich. The collaboration will leverage Bosch's factories and logistics centers in China to build a complete "real data — embodied model — real scenario" technology loop, deploying at scale in high-precision industrial environments like automotive manufacturing within two years.

Bosch will supply Spirit AI with critical robot components including actuators and sensors, supporting product development, engineering validation, and mass production. Spirit AI's Spirit v1.5 embodied foundation model has surpassed the international benchmark Pi0.5, reaching global leading performance.

Both sides described the partnership as "a critical milestone from technology leadership to industry leadership," introducing a new ecosystem paradigm for the robotics sector.

Global industrial giant (Bosch) partnering with Chinese AI startup (Spirit AI) signals embodied AI's entry into industrial deployment phase
